Bezier Curves

About bezier curves

Applications in computing

When drawing curves in programs like photoshop, illustrate or other graphic design programs there is generally a 'pen tool' allowing you to draw fluid lines on your canvas that are easily adjustable.

How they are generated

The computer defines 4 points, illustrated by the four squares on the canvas above. It then picks lines between the given points at a certain factor, then traces the path of the new midpoint as the factor increases.
A good representation of the math behind this can be found here on the MIT website.
